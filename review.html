Ruth1999

Gives you insight into the person..the movie doesn't really declare her innocence or guilt. But it does make you realize that there's always a story and real people behind every headline. In a way, you feel compassion for her and her situation. It's also an indictment of the US Skating Association, an elitist group that will only allow a certain type of person, i.e. Affluent, "nice", "all American" girls, to represent the country in the sport. Any sport that's based on subjective judging will never be fair because it's never just about the athlete's abilities. There's always politics and bias.
